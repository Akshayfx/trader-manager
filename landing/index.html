Features

Everything You Need to Trade Like a Pro

Powerful tools designed to streamline your trading workflow and protect your capital

Plan Trade

Set precise entry, stop loss, and take profit levels with visual lines on your chart. Auto-calculates lot size based on your risk parameters.

  • Visual SL/TP lines on chart
  • Auto direction detection
  • Risk % or $ risk options

Partial Take Profit

Set multiple TP levels to scale out of positions gradually. Close percentages at each level to lock in profits.

  • Multiple TP levels
  • Pips or price-based triggers
  • Visual lines on chart

Auto Breakeven

Automatically move your stop loss to breakeven when price moves in your favor. Protect your trades with zero risk.

  • Custom trigger pips
  • Plus pips buffer option
  • SL at entry or in profit

AI Trade Analysis

Get detailed weekly and monthly reports analyzing your trading performance. AI identifies patterns and suggests improvements.

  • Win rate analysis
  • Best/worst pair insights
  • Personalized recommendations

Prop Firm Mode

Protect your prop firm challenge with daily loss limits, news blocking, and automatic trade closure when limits are hit.

  • Daily loss limit protection
  • News-based trade blocking
  • Auto-close on limit hit

News Filter

Stay informed about upcoming economic events with real-time news alerts. Customize which currencies and impact levels to monitor.

  • Economic calendar integration
  • Customizable currency pairs
  • Impact level filtering
